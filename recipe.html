mac and cheese recipe

ingrediants

  • macaroni
  • cheese
  • garlic powder
  • whole milk
  • salted butter
  • flour

steps to perpare

  1. first you boil the water and make sure to put a generous amount of salt into the water then cook the macaroni for 10 minutes.
  2. after cooked to a perfect texure you will drain but do not rinse
  3. now time to make the cheese sauce we will start it out with a roux and flour to create a thick sauce start by letting the butter melt. (not brown)
  4. then whisk in the dry ingredieants (flour, salt and garlic powder) for about 60 seconds.
  5. after the roux has cooked for a minute add the milk and whisk really well, then add the sour cream. (you can also use greek yougert)
  6. now we will cook this over medium head whisking often untill sauce starts to thickin. (DO NOT LET THIS MIXURE BOIL)
  7. add the cheese and whisk untill smooth for about 1-2 minutes.
  8. add the macaroni into the cheese sauce and stir untill even and smooth.

toppings or variations

  • you can top with a sprinkle of chicken or any meat
  • also can top with other types of crackers
  • another intresting one is crab chips is a great topping!
